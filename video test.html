Camber exploded

This is how Camber was fabricated: it has four main components: the polished stainless steel frame, the natural single-piece seat, the stainless steel screws, and the zinc alloy tapping inserts. The frame was handmade locally in California, and the seat was CNCed at ArtCenter. The hardwares are from McMaster-Carr.The seat is available in walnut, oak, and cork.
